Game Instructions

Objective: Be the first player to get enough of your pieces in a row to win.

How to Play

  • Select a game mode from the main menu and press "Start Game".
  • Players take turns placing their pieces (Red or Blue) into the grid.
  • Two-Step Placement: Click once on a valid column to select it, then click again to confirm and place your piece.
  • Rotate Cube (Gravity Shift): Use the "Rotate Cube" buttons to change the direction of gravity. This will cause all pieces on the board to shift.

Controls

  • Mouse Drag: Click and drag to rotate your view of the cube.
  • Reset View: Returns your camera view to the default starting position.
  • Undo Move: Reverts the last move.
  • Back to Menu: Ends the current game and returns to the main menu.

Good luck!
